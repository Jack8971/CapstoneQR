Tinkersculpt

A web-based tool built for Capstone Dynamics that helps teams plan and track how their people's time is being used across projects. See who is assigned to what and how much capacity they have available. Track initiatives from creation through to completion. View monthly allocation breakdowns by team member and category. Role-based access for Resource Managers, Stakeholders, and Team Members.
